Steve Jobs

Co-founder of Apple Inc.

Februrary 24, 1955 - October 5, 2011

A simple timeline:

  • 1986 - Cold calling Bill HewLett to ask for electronic parts, landed summer job @ HP
  • 1974 - Lands job with Atari due to Steve Wozniak's "Pong" game created in 1973. Atari thought Steve was the one who created the game.
  • 1975 - Atari video game "Breakout" TTL chips reduced to 46 by Steve Wozniak. Jobs and Wozniak split the money given by Atari.
  • 1976 - The first design of the Apple I. This lead to the creation of the Apple Computer Company with Jobs, Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne.
  • 1977 - Introduction of the Apple II. The Apple II was one of the first highly successful mass produced microcomputer products in the world.
  • 1984 - Introduction of Macintosh.
  • 1985 - Jobs resigns from Apple. Founds NeXT Inc.
  • 1986 - Jobs funds the spinout of The Graphics Group, which was later named Pixar.
  • 1997 - Jobs rejoins Apple.
  • 2001 - Introduction of the iPod.
  • 2007 - Introduction of the iPhone.
